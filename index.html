About Me

Throughout my life, I can always recall having a passion for science and the arts. The pursuit of engineering, by no coincidence, has been one of my most enlightening and defining experiences. However, as I matured and became aware of the state of the world around me, I began to realize that engineers bear an incredible responsibility. No matter the industry I found myself in, I was always faced with decisions that made a difference. My mission is to ensure that the difference I make is a positive one.

Today, I am winding-down a short but impactful stint as a product developer. While I honed many skills in design, communication, and collaboration, ultimately I'm eager to find a role where I can more definitively have a positive impact on quality of life around the world. Until then, I'll continue to hone my skills by pursing challenging new projects with critical peers.

I hope you have the time to explore and enjoy this portfolio. If you have additional questions about my interests or experiences, I'd enjoy hearing from you. My resume and contact information are available below.

Technical Skills

Communication

I am an articulate communicator, capable of distilling complex technical concepts into simple terms for all audiences. Clear and thorough; whether I'm illustrating possible solutions to clients, planning strategy with internal stakeholders, or conveying key technical information to external partners.

Collaboration

As a natural collaborator, I excel as part of small teams with organized goals. I am incredibly motivated when I can help others to achieve their goals.

Problem Definition

I am a fast learner who quickly understands key technical subcomponents of a given problem, and possess a unique aptitude for identifying the most likely 'sticking points' early, enabling focused development of solutions.

Solution Development

My product development experience honed an ability to methodically work through a problem and generate possible solutions, evaluating them separately and as a whole while explore the landscape of possible solutions.

Theoretical Appraisal

My background in mechanical engineering has allowed me to consistently demonstrate my analytical and computational mindset, and my intuition for mechanical systems aids in efficently exploring the limits of a design. I am comfortable leveraging and learning new analytical tools, such as CAD, FEA, or programming languages, and generally integrate them into my toolkit with ease.

Personal Drive
