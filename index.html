About Me

Throughout my life, I can always recall having a passion for science, technology, and the arts. The pursuit of engineering, by no coincidence, has proven to be one of my most enlightening and defining experiences. However, as I matured and became aware of the state of the world around me, I began to realize that my career path constitutes incredible responsibilities. No matter the industry I find myself in, I will always be faced with decisions that will make a difference. My mission is to ensure that the difference I make is a positive one.

Today, I am a production engineer at N12 Technologies working with a small team to launch our company's first industrial-scale manufacturing operation in Dayton, OH. Previously at N12, I found success managing our pilot CVD reactor, as well as developing in-house engineering solutions for quality control and machine upgrades. Today, I'm faced with the fresh challenge of managing external resources, coordinating technical operations, and forecasting budgetary and production capacity needs based on annual goals.

I hope you have the time to explore and enjoy this portfolio. If you have additional questions about my interests or experiences, I'd enjoy hearing from you. My resume and contact information are available below.
