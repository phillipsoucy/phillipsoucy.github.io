About Me

Throughout my life, I can always recall having a passion for science, technology, and the arts. The pursuit of engineering, by no coincidence, has proven to be one of my most enlightening and defining experiences. However, as I matured and became aware of the state of the world around me, I began to realize that my career path is replete with incredible responsibilities. No matter the industry I find myself in, I will always be faced with decisions that will make a difference. My mission is to ensure that the difference I make is a positive one.

Today, I am a mechanical engineer at MAKE Composites working with a small team to launch an automated, desktop-scale manufacturing system for continuous-fiber composites. One day, I'd like to work in a role where I can design, engineer, and create to enabling people to do things that would be otherwise difficult, frustrating, or physically impossible for them. Until then, I'll continue to hone my skills by pursing challenging projects with critical peers.

I hope you have the time to explore and enjoy this portfolio. If you have additional questions about my interests or experiences, I'd enjoy hearing from you. My resume and contact information are available below.
